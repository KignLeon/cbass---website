Core Capabilities

Expert Electrical
Services

From heavy-up panel upgrades to precision troubleshooting. We provide safe, code-compliant solutions for your home or business.

01. Upgrade

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Is your panel outdated, buzzing, or warm to the touch? We specialize in 100A to 200A heavy-ups, fuse box replacements, and sub-panel installations. Ensure your home has the power capacity for modern appliances, AC, and EV chargers.

02. Repair

Troubleshooting & Repair

Flickering lights? Dead outlets? Breaker tripping repeatedly? Our expert troubleshooting finds the root cause fast. We trace circuits, identify faults, and repair them safely to prevent fire hazards.

03. Install

Lighting & Fans

Transform your home with modern lighting. We install recessed lighting (can lights), chandeliers, pendant lights, under-cabinet lighting, and ceiling fans (even on high ceilings).

04. EV Power

EV Charger Installation

Get Level 2 charging at home. We install dedicated circuits for Tesla Wall Connectors, NEMA 14-50 outlets, and other EV chargers. We handle the load calculation to ensure your panel can handle it.
